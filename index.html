WHY JOIN US?

View light Years Away!

Come join both experts and novices, with a common enthusiasm for astronomy, giving you live views, video footage and full explanation of the sky above and the various cellestial objects in orbit above and beyond.

Witness Cellestial Events!

Come along and see the birth of new stars, comets and other cosmic events that will not be seen again for thousands of years to come, all through the naked eye and wiht the help of telescopes.

Access to Resources for Free!

With the help and guidance of our speakers, you will gain free access to mobile apps and many other resources to supplement your interest in astronomy.

Meet People!

Meet the group over a coffee at the beginning of each Star-gazing meet-up, knowing we all share a common interest in astronomy and the night sky above.
